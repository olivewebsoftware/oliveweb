Rules

No spamming

Do not send the same messages over and over again.

Don’t be an idiot

Do not send unhelpful or stupid things in chat rooms.

Don’t be a jerk

Do not harass somebody or out someone because of things like race, religion, sexuality, etc. It’s not cool.

No scamming

Don’t try to scam people or IM people with the intent to scam them.

Read the room

If everybody else in the chat doesn’t seem to be paying any attention to the wall of text you sent, it probably means nobody cares.

Don’t be a creep

Don’t ask people for personal information like names, addresses, phone numbers, etc.

No illegal activity

You are not allowed to do things that are against the law in our chat rooms.

No copyrighted material

Do not send copyrighted material that you don’t have the rights to use.

Stay on topic

Stay on the topic of the chat room that you are in. If you want to discuss another topic, join another chat room.

Failure to abide by these rules may result in account deletion.
