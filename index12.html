Welcome to the app submission tool for our app store!
Type the name of your app, what your developer name is in the email window.
IMPORTANT! After pressing 'Submit', an email window will pop up.
Do not edit anything in the subject of this email. In the body part of
the email, attach a ZIP file containing all of your app files. Also, you can
include anything else you would like us to know about your app. Make sure
the app is an HTML file. Thank you for submitting an app to us and making
your app usable by everyone.


In the email, include these items:
1. Name of app
2. Developer of app
3. ZIP file containing all the files for the app (the app itself must be written in html)
4. An icon for the app
We will scan the file for viruses once we recieve it. There is no fee for submitting apps, and you can
submit as many as you want. Apps may take up to 48 hours to get onto the store. If your app gets onto the
store, we will publish it and let you know that your app was published onto the store. If it was not, we will
let you know that it wasn't published and tell you why. If you have any questions, please contact our support!
Thanks for choosing OliveWeb!
