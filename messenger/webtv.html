Introducing OliveWeb Messenger

OliveWeb Messenger is an online instant messaging service
from OliveWeb! It has instant messages, chat rooms, contact
management, and more!


Where can I get OliveWeb Messenger?

On a PC with Windows, visit https://olivewebsoftware.com/messenger
and download the application.


Supported Operating Systems:

Windows 11, 10, 8, and 7
